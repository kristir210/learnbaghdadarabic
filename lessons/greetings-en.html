Common Greetings in Baghdad Arabic

Learn essential phrases for daily interactions:

  • 1. Hello/Hi: مرحبا marhaba (neutral)
  • 2. Good morning: صباح الخير sabaħ al-khayr (neutral)
  • 3. Good evening: مساء الخير masā’ al-khayr (neutral)
  • 4. Goodbye: مع السلامة ma’a as-salāma (neutral)
  • 5. How are you?: شلونك؟ shlawnak? (male) / shlawnich? (female)
  • 6. I’m fine, thank you: أنا بخير، شكرًا ana bikhayr, shukran (male) / ani bikhayr, shukran (female)
  • 7. Pleased to meet you: سعادت أني قابلتك sa’ādat ʿani qābaltek (neutral)
  • 8. Welcome: أهلا وسهلا ahlan wa sahlan (neutral)
  • 9. Peace be upon you: السلام عليكم as-salām ʿalaykum (plural) / as-salām ʿalayk (singular male) / as-salām ʿalaych (singular female)
  • 10. And also on you: وعليكم السلام wa ʿalaykum as-salām (plural) / wa ʿalayk as-salām (singular male) / wa ʿalaych as-salām (singular female)
  • 11. God keep you safe: الله يعافيك Allah yishkurak (male) / Allah yishkurich (female)
  • 12. Good morning (informal): صباح الخير sabaħ al-khayr ya sadiqi (male) / sabaħ al-khayr ya sadiqti (female)
  • 13. Good evening (informal): مساء الخير masā’ al-khayr ya sadiqi (male) / masā’ al-khayr ya sadiqti (female)
  • 14. How are you today?: شلونك اليوم؟ shlawnak al-yawm? (male) / shlawnich al-yawm? (female)
  • 15. Things are good: تحياتي tayyib (neutral) or zayn (colloquial Baghdadi)
  • 16. May God guide you: الله يهديك Allah yahdik (male) / Allah yahdich (female)
  • 17. God bless you: الله يباركلك Allah yibāraklik (male) / Allah yibāraklich (female)
  • 18. How are the family?: إيش شايف العيلة؟ shlawn al-ʿayla? (gender-neutral, colloquial)
  • 19. Goodbye (to a group): إلى اللقاء ilā al-liqā’ (neutral)
  • 20. May peace be with you all: السلام عليكم جميعاً as-salām ʿalaykum jamīʿan (plural)
