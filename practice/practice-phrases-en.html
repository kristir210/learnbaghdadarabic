Pronunciation Guide: Common Phrases

Practice saying these phrases aloud:

  • 1. Excuse me / Sorry: عذراً izizin (say: iz-ZEE-zin)
  • 2. Thank you very much: شكراً جزيلاً shukran jazīlan (say: shoo-KRAHN jah-ZEE-lan)
  • 3. You’re welcome: عفواً afwan (say: AHF-wahn)
  • 4. I don’t understand (male): ما فاهم ma fahim (say: mah fah-HEEM)
  • 5. I don’t understand (female): ما فاهمة ma fahima (say: mah fah-HEE-mah)
  • 6. Can you repeat that? (male): تكرر؟ tikrar? (say: tik-RAHR?)
  • 7. Can you repeat that? (female): تكرري؟ tikrari? (say: tik-RAH-ree?)
  • 8. What does this mean?: ويش معناها؟ wish maʿnahā? (say: weesh mah-NAH-ha?)
  • 9. I’m hungry (male): جوعان jūʿān (say: joo-AHN)
  • 10. I’m hungry (female): جوعانة jūʿāna (say: joo-AH-nah)
  • 11. I’m thirsty (male): عطشان ʿaṭashān (say: at-TAH-shan)
  • 12. I’m thirsty (female): عطشانة ʿaṭashāna (say: at-TAH-shah-nah)
  • 13. Where is the bathroom?: وين الحمام؟ wayn al-ḥammām? (say: wayn al-ham-MAM?)
  • 14. How much is this?: شقد هاي؟ shkadd hāyi? (say: shkad-DAY hi?)
  • 15. I want this (male): أريد هاي arīd hāyi (say: ah-REED hi)
  • 16. I want this (female): أريدة هاي arīda hāyi (say: ah-REE-dah hi)
  • 17. Can you help me? (male): تساعدني؟ tisāʿidnī? (say: tee-SAHD-nee?)
  • 18. Can you help me? (female): تساعدني؟ tisāʿidnī? (say: tee-SAHD-nee?)
  • 19. I don’t know (male): ما أدري ma adrī (say: mah ad-REE)
  • 20. I don’t know (female): ما أدرية ma adrīya (say: mah ad-REE-yah)
  • 21. It’s okay / No problem: ماكو مشكلة mākū mushkila (say: mah-KOO moosh-KEE-lah)
  • 22. Let’s go: نروح nirūḥ (say: nee-ROOH)
  • 23. Wait a moment: استنا شوي istannā shway (say: iss-tah-NAH shway)
  • 24. Come here (male): تعاله taʿālā (say: tah-AL-lah)
  • 25. Come here (female): تعالي taʿālī (say: tah-ah-LEE)
  • 26. Be careful: انتبه intabih (say: een-TAH-beeh)
  • 27. What’s wrong?: ويش المشكلة؟ wish al-mushkila? (say: weesh al-moosh-KEE-lah?)
  • 28. Take care (male): استر على حالك istr ʿalā ḥālik (say: ISS-ter ah-LAH hah-leek)
  • 29. Take care (female): استر على حالچ istr ʿalā ḥālich (say: ISS-ter ah-LAH hah-leesh)
  • 30. What does this mean?: ويش معناها؟ wish maʿnahā? (say: weesh mah-NAH-ha?)
