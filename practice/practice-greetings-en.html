Pronunciation Guide: Common Greetings

Practice saying these phrases aloud:

  • 1. Hello/Hi: مرحبا marhaba (say: mahr-HAH-bah)
  • 2. Good morning: صباح الخير sabaħ al-khayr (say: sah-BAH al-KHEE-yer)
  • 3. Good evening: مساء الخير masā’ al-khayr (say: mah-SAH al-KHEE-yer)
  • 4. Goodbye: مع السلامة ma’a as-salāma (say: mah-ah sah-LAH-mah)
  • 5. How are you? (male): شلونك؟ shlawnak? (say: shlawn-NOOK?)
  • 6. How are you? (female): شلونچ؟ shlawnich? (say: shlawn-NEECH?)
  • 7. I’m fine, thank you (male): أنا بخير، شكرًا ana bikhayr, shukran (say: AH-nah bee-KHAIR, shoo-KRAHN)
  • 8. I’m fine, thank you (female): أنا بخير، شكرًا ani bikhayr, shukran (say: A-nee bee-KHAIR, shoo-KRAHN)
  • 9. Peace be upon you (plural): السلام عليكم as-salām ʿalaykum (say: ah-sah-LAHM ah-LAY-koom)
  • 10. Peace be upon you (singular male): السلام عليك as-salām ʿalayk (say: ah-sah-LAHM ah-LAY-ek)
  • 11. Peace be upon you (singular female): السلام عليچ as-salām ʿalaych (say: ah-sah-LAHM ah-LAY-esh)
  • 12. And also on you (plural): وعليكم السلام wa ʿalaykum as-salām (say: wah ah-LAY-koom ah-sah-LAHM)
  • 13. And also on you (singular male): وعليك السلام wa ʿalayk as-salām (say: wah ah-LAY-ek ah-sah-LAHM)
  • 14. And also on you (singular female): وعليچ السلام wa ʿalaych as-salām (say: wah ah-LAY-esh ah-sah-LAHM)
  • 15. God keep you safe (male): الله يعافيك Allah yishkurak (say: AH-lah yee-SH-koo-rak)
  • 16. God keep you safe (female): الله يعافيك Allah yishkurich (say: AH-lah yee-SH-koo-reesh)
  • 17. Good morning (informal, male): صباح الخير sabaħ al-khayr ya sadiqi (say: sah-BAH al-KHEE-yer yah sah-DEE-kee)
  • 18. Good morning (informal, female): صباح الخير sabaħ al-khayr ya sadiqti (say: sah-BAH al-KHEE-yer yah sah-DEE-tee)
  • 19. Good evening (informal, male): مساء الخير masā’ al-khayr ya sadiqi (say: mah-SAH al-KHEE-yer yah sah-DEE-kee)
  • 20. Good evening (informal, female): مساء الخير masā’ al-khayr ya sadiqti (say: mah-SAH al-KHEE-yer yah sah-DEE-tee)
  • 21. How are you today? (male): شلونك اليوم؟ shlawnak al-yawm? (say: shlawn-NOOK al-YOWM?)
  • 22. How are you today? (female): شلونچ اليوم؟ shlawnich al-yawm? (say: shlawn-NEECH al-YOWM?)
  • 23. Things are good: تحياتي tayyib (say: TIE-yib) or zayn (colloquial: ZAYN)
  • 24. May God guide you (male): الله يهديك Allah yahdik (say: AH-lah yah-DEEK)
  • 25. May God guide you (female): الله يهديك Allah yahdich (say: AH-lah yah-DEESH)
  • 26. God bless you (male): الله يباركلك Allah yibāraklik (say: AH-lah yee-BAR-ak-leek)
  • 27. God bless you (female): الله يباركلك Allah yibāraklich (say: AH-lah yee-BAR-ak-leesh)
  • 28. How are the family? (gender-neutral): شلون العيلة؟ shlawn al-ʿayla? (say: shlawn al-AY-lah?)
  • 29. Goodbye (to a group): إلى اللقاء ilā al-liqā’ (say: ee-LAH al-lee-THA)
  • 30. May peace be with you all: السلام عليكم جميعاً as-salām ʿalaykum jamīʿan (say: ah-sah-LAHM ah-LAY-koom jah-MEE-awn)
